\begin{frame}{Scalability}
\textbf{Theory:}
The error of $\wmle_i$ and $\wave$ scales as $O(1/\sqrt{n})$.

\hspace{0.55in} The error of $\wowa$ and $\wmle$ scales as $O(1/\sqrt{mn})$.

\hspace{0.55in} The error of $\wboot$ scales as $O(1/\sqrt{mn} + 1/\sqrt{n^3})$.
